Applying for Subscription
 Previous Top Next

You can apply for a selected course in the institute, upload required documents and choose to renew the course automatically. The application emails these details to the selected institute.

You can view the following:

1.      Requester: Displays the name of the institute.

2.      Subscription: Displays the name of the course.

3.      Renew Automatically: Select the check box to renew your subscription automatically at the end of the billing cycle.

4.      Notes: Enter additional details, if any.

5.      Documents: Click to upload required documents.

6.      Click [Apply] to apply for the subscription or click to cancel. Bixera displays a message indicating that the application is submitted successfully when you click [Apply].
